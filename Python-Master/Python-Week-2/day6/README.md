# Day 6: Marathon & Mock Test (Stress Testing)

## Overview
Day 6 is designed to simulate competition or interview pressure. You will apply the patterns learned over the past 5 days (Two Pointers, Sliding Window, Strings/Hashing, and Matrices) under strict time constraints.

## Learning Objectives
1. Build endurance by solving 20 problems back-to-back in the Marathon Set.
2. Experience simulated interview pressure in a 10-problem Timed Mock Test.
3. Quantify weaknesses using the Performance Tracking and Mistake Classification templates.

## Structure
- `marathon_set/`: 20 mixed-topic problems to solve in a 4-hour block.
- `mock_test/`: A 90-minute strict timed test of 10 problems.
- `performance_tracking/`: Rubrics for analyzing time spent, bugs, and pattern recognition failures.
- `stress_review/`: A psychological/technical checklist to review post-test.
